About Me

I’m a 26 year old professional golfer from Huddersfield, England. I play out of Meltham GC and Woodsome Hall GC, both in West Yorkshire. I currently play on the UK based PGA Europro Tour. I come from a family full of golf fanatics! My dad is a very accomplished player (playing off a handicap of 4), and both my grandads have been very keen golfers for a long time.

I managed to achieve many of the ambitions I had growing up, during my amateur career. I represented England, travelled the world and won national and international tournaments. But I have many goals still to achieve. I want to play and win on the European Tour, PGA Tour, win majors, and become the best player I can be. My hobbies off the course include playing guitar, watching football (Arsenal if you were wondering!) and I have also recently started to learn web development.

My Team

Underpin Sports

Management

Underpin is a sports performance agency founded and run by Greg Robertson and Duncan McCarthy. I’ve known both Greg and Duncan for several years now and it was a very easy decision to sign with Underpin at the start of 2019. Since then, I’ve found it invaluable having the combination of Greg’s business expertise and Duncan’s wealth of experience in performance coaching to help guide me through the early stages of my professional career. Their player first approach helps me identify ways to maximise my potential both on and off the course.

Simon Race

Coach

Simon is the Head Professional at my home course Meltham Golf Club. He has done a superb job in this role since he started there in 2013, and he is held in extremely high regard by the members. Simon has been coaching me since April 2019, and the work we’ve done since then has seen great improvements in my long game. His passion and commitment to coaching is second to none and I look forward to making great strides under his guidance again this year.

Andy Young

Fitness

Andy is a Chartered Physiotherapist and runs his own practice in Holmfirth (A P Young Physiotherapy). I’ve also worked with Andy since April 2019. He provides me with a tailored program which helps me ensure my body is capable of doing the things I’m working on in my golf swing, whilst also maintaining a good base level of strength and fitness. I also see him regularly for treatment during the season, to ensure I stay injury free as much as possible. Andy is a true expert in his field and a crucial part of my team.
